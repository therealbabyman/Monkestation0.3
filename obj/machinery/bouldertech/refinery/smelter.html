smelter
/obj/machinery/bouldertech/refinery/smelter

Your other new favorite industrial waste magnet! Accepts boulders and produces sheets of metalic materials. Can be upgraded with stock parts or through chemical inputs. When upgraded, it can hold more boulders and process more at once.

Chemical inputs can be used to boost the refinery's efficiency, but produces industrial waste, which eats through the station and is generally difficult to store.
